Training On Jewellery Design and Marketing For SME’s In The Jewellery Sector


The training on Jewellery Design and Marketing for SME’s in the Jewellery Sector is a Participants of “Training and Capacity Building to Increase MSMEs Competitiveness on Jewellery Design” Project that is conducted by the Centre for Handicraft and Batik, Ministry of Industry of Indonesia in Yogyakarta, Indonesia and funded by AHKFTA ECOTECH Work Programme.

The training implementation is projected to have a positive impact on the participants in the form of enhanced capacity and capability, which will in turn, improve the quality of jewellery produced by SMEs in ASEAN and contribute to a positive societal impact among the SME’s actors in the jewellery industry through knowledge sharing among them.



Training Participants

In total, there will be 22 participants for The Training on Jewellery Design and Marketing for SME’s in the Jewellery Sector. Two participants from each of ASEAN member countries that are also members of AHKTDC , such as (Brunei Darussalam, Cambodia, Indonesia, Lao PDR, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Viet Nam) also Hong Kong . We require all participants to be running a jewellery business or at least having three years of experience in the jewellery business and willing to enter the Hong Kong jewellery market.

Training Objectives

Habits and Behaviors of Jewelry Users

Increase participants' understanding of the habits and behaviour of jewellery users in Hong Kong.

Technical Skills Design and Modeling jewellery

Improving the technical ability to design jewellery up to jewellery wax modeling to meet the style and demand of the jewellery market in Hong Kong.

Standards Requirements Trade Jewelry

Increase participants' understanding of the standards and requirements for entering the jewellery trade in Hong Kong.
